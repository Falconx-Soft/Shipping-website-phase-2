Who we are?

Ship Chandler Laem Chabang Co., Ltd is your premier ship supply agency in the bustling Port Laem Chabang, dedicated to providing top-notch services to the maritime industry. As a trusted ship chandler, we specialize in ship provision, offering a comprehensive range of supplies for vessels docking in Port Laem Chabang. We understand the unique needs of ships, and our mission is to ensure that their provisions, equipment, and services are readily available to keep them operating smoothly.

Our commitment to excellence extends to our fresh food supplies for ships, ensuring that the maritime community receives quality, nutritious provisions while in Port Laem Chabang. Ship Chandler Laem Chabang Co., Ltd takes pride in being a reliable partner for ships seeking food supplies and a wide array of ship services. Whether it's ship supply, ship provisioning, or any other ship-related service, our team is dedicated to meeting your needs with efficiency and professionalism. We look forward to serving you and enhancing your experience at Port Laem Chabang.
